About course


How often do you admire the works of contemporary illustrators in magazines, books or even cups of coffee and catch yourself thinking: "Would I learn so much!"? Do you think it's the lot of rare lucky people to create illustrations and get paid for it? In fact, this is the result of a lot of work. Illustrator can be anyone who is ready to spend enough time and effort to train.

What will give you a course:

1 Find out what the work of the modern illustrator is.
2 Meet different techniques and types of graphic and pictorial means of expressiveness.
3 Find out what stylization is and what its types exist. Learn to use them in your work.
4 Learn to create whole worlds in which your characters will live and act.
5 Learn how to convey the mood and character of the characters, how to properly place the characters in their work.
6 Find your own style that not only makes your work recognizable, but also highlights your strengths.
